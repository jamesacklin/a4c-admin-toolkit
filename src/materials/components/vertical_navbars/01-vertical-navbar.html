--- notes: | Vertical navbars are useful for creating sidebars with the same pattern and functionality of horizontal, page-header navbars. Vertical navbars will expand to 100% width, so make sure they are contained within a column (`.col-sm-4` in this example) next to a main content column to give the appearance of a sidebar. Vertical navbars will also collapse to a responsive, expanding menu on mobile devices and small screens (<768px). Vertical navbars also accept the same `.navbar-inverse`, `.navbar-branded`, and `.navbar-branded-inverse` classes and `data-brand` attributes as horizontal ones. ---
